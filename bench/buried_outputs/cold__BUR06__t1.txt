Sorting 5GB before grep is your bottleneck — sort is O(n log n) and I/O-heavy; grep is O(n) and streaming. Remove the sort entirely. `grep -c ERROR huge.log` is already optimal (single pass, no temp files). If you must parallelize: `split -l 1000000 huge.log chunk_ && grep -c ERROR chunk_* | awk '{sum+=$1} END{print sum}'` or use `rg -c ERROR huge.log` (ripgrep, parallel by default). `xargs -P` adds overhead for line-oriented work — avoid.
