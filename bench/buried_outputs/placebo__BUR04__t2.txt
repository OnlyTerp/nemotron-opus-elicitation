Loading 8 GB CSV in `__getitem__` is your bottleneck — `num_workers` and `pin_memory` are irrelevant. Fix: preprocess once. Convert to Parquet/Arrow/HDF5 with row groups, or use `pandas.read_csv(..., chunksize=...)` to build an index, then memory-map. Or load the whole DF once in `__init__` if RAM allows. Then `__getitem__` is a cheap iloc lookup. Only then tune DataLoader: `num_workers=4–8`, `pin_memory=True`, `persistent_workers=True`.
