Loading the entire 8GB CSV in `__getitem__` is your bottleneck — it's O(n) per sample. Fix the Dataset: load the CSV once in `__init__` (or use memory-mapped formats like Parquet/Feather). Then `num_workers > 0` and `pin_memory=True` will help by parallelizing I/O and enabling async GPU transfers. Start with `num_workers=4` (CPU cores / 2) and `pin_memory=True`. But the CSV reload must go first — no DataLoader tuning fixes O(n²) data loading.
