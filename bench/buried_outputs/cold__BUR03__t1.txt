Opening a new connection per INSERT is your bottleneck — connection overhead dominates. Use a connection pool (e.g., `psycopg2.pool.ThreadedConnectionPool`) or persistent connections. Partitions won't help until you fix this. For write scaling: batch INSERTs (multi-row `INSERT ... VALUES (...), (...)`), use `COPY` for bulk loads, or consider a write-ahead log table. Partition by time (e.g., monthly) only if you have >100M rows and need partition pruning for queries.
